iHRIS Train is a human resources management tool that enables an organization to track and manage a comprehensive human resources strategy for In-service Trainings. While primarily intended for tracking health care worker trainings, iHRIS Train may be readily adapted to other types of workforces and trainings. iHRIS Train is a free, Open Source software solution developed by The Capacity Project, an innovative global initiative to help developing countries build and sustain the health workforce.
